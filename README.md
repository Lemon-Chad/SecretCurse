# Secret Curses

## Gameplay

Users choose three pieces to build their crew.

Each piece has
- Movement pattern
- Attack pattern
- Stats
    - Health
    - Speed (Lockout time after moving)
    - Damage
- Activation pattern (spawning condition)

Users start with their "primary piece" (chosen out of their three) on opposite sides of the board from their opponent.

Users can move and attack with pieces, collect
resources on the board, and perform activation
patterns to summon their other two pieces.

Win by eliminating all other pieces.
